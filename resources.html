Quick LNAT Success Tips

Essential strategies from LNAT experts

Read Actively, Not Passively

Engage with the passage by identifying key arguments, assumptions, and evidence. Don't just read - analyze as you go.

Practice Time Management Daily

Aim for 2.2 minutes per question. Practice with a timer until this becomes natural and automatic.

Focus on Question Stems

Read the question carefully before the answer choices. Know exactly what you're looking for before evaluating options.

Eliminate Wrong Answers First

Use process of elimination. Often it's easier to spot wrong answers than to immediately identify the correct one.

Don't Second-Guess Yourself

Trust your first instinct, especially if you've prepared thoroughly. Changing answers often leads to mistakes.

Practice Under Real Conditions

Simulate actual test conditions: quiet environment, proper timing, no breaks. Build stamina for the real exam.
